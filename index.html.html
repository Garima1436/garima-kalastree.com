About Me

I'm Garima Awasthi

"I believe the hands creating India's heritage deserve global recognition, financial independence, and access to modern digital tools."

I'm a PhD researcher, author, and women empowerment advocate passionate about preserving India's cultural heritage while creating economic opportunities for women through technology and innovation.

My research at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT Patna combines FinTech, women empowerment, and GI-tagged products to explore how traditional women artisans can grow in the digital economy — from Madhubani painters in Bihar to Pashmina weavers in Kashmir.

My Research Focus

Empowering Women Through
GI Product Ecosystems

My research theme: "Empowering Women with FinTech Access in GI-Tagged Product Ecosystems" — exploring the digital-financial frontier for India's women artisans across 16 states.

🌾

GI Registry Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of 478 Geographical Indication tags registered from 2004–2023. Linear regression: GI_count = −1132.73 + 0.574 × Year (p<0.001).

478GI Tags · 32 States

FinTech Adoption

Primary survey of 2,500 women across 16 states measuring UPI, AEPS, microloan apps, digital marketplace, insurtech, and digital bookkeeping adoption rates.

0.527Avg FinTech Index
🤝

Women's Empowerment

Measuring empowerment through income control, household decision participation, mobility independence, and self-efficacy scores across GI value chains.

0.393Avg Empowerment Index
🏛️

Policy & Barriers

Identification of 3 primary barriers: digital literacy (47.4%), trust & fraud concerns (32.8%), device access (28.9%). Policy recommendations for SHG-led digital onboarding.
